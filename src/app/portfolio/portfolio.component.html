Legal Bot

Chat bot product from Westernacher, that can be used by other customers for their customer support needs. Targeted for legal and justice related offices in Germany primarily.

Initial Research

We conducted interviews and discussions with stake holders about the real problem that our customers were facing, as an organisation Westernacher has products that serve the needs of judiciary,Notary and lawyers mostly in Germany. With a chat bot application we wished to provide a solution where Notaries and lawyers can have an automated chat solution where they can deal with any questions from their clients and provide a solution either by an automated reply which has trained models or by connecting to a support person who can provide a solution.

Ideation

After getting all the data from stake holders we planned for a data flow for our automated chat bot system and data Structure in which we will store data in data base and Json structure that Backend would provide to Front End


My Role

In a team of four members, my role is primarily a Front-End developer, I convert designs and ,prototypes that designers provide into application and also contributing in designing of screens that are responsive and scalable.
I have worked on technologies like JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Angular v9, GraphJs, Angular Material


Result

This application is in its final leg with a couple of successful presentations made to stakeholders and higher management, we have provided this application as a library which requires minimum configuration.

Travel Management

Travel Management is an in house application developed for the Westernacher team to record and track travel request applications

Initial Research

We conducted interviews and discussions with stake holders about the problem and the expectations, Westernacher having its presence globally and growing rapidly requires a lot of travel across the globe for official works

Ideation

After getting all the requirements from stake holders we came up with a few simple screens that could enable user to navigate trough screens

This design provided initial functionality but an edit to a form required user to travel back and forth using next and previous button, The newer design provides a better approach when user knows his active tab and is easy to traverse.


My Role

In a team of four members, my role is primarily a Front-End developer and Designer, I made pen and paper prototype of the screens designing a few steppers and tab view to traverse application. I was also responsible for converting these designs to reality on screens with a working functionality, integrating with backend to perform CRUD operation.
I have worked on technologies like JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Angular v9, Bootstrap v4


Result

The application provides ability to the employees to raise a travel request and upload their relevant documents, it also allows them to track the reques for approval and an email notification is send every time status is updated
